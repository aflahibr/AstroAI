Love Guidance

- Emotional clarity strengthens relationships.
- Try communicating your feelings openly.
- Avoid arguments driven by ego or impatience.
- Show appreciation for the small gestures.
- Venus is the planet of love and relationships.
- Its placement in the 7th house heavily influences marriage and partnerships.
- Mars in the 7th house can cause friction (Kuja Dosha/Manglik).
- Moon's influence on the 4th house affects emotional bonding and domestic harmony.
- Jupiter aspecting the 7th house brings blessings and growth in partnerships.
