Vedic Astrology Core Concepts
The 12 Houses:
1st House: Self, appearance, identity.
2nd House: Wealth, family, speech.
3rd House: Courage, siblings, communication.
4th House: Mother, home, inner peace.
5th House: Children, intellect, romance, creativity.
6th House: Debt, disease, enemies, daily work.
7th House: Marriage, partnerships, business.
8th House: Longevity, transformations, occult.
9th House: Luck, religion, father, higher education.
10th House: Career, profession, karma, public standing.
11th House: Gains, friends, fulfillment of desires.
12th House: Losses, expenses, spirituality, foreign lands.

Career Guidance:
Jupiter aspecting the 10th house brings luck and expansion in career.
Saturn in the 10th house brings slow but steady progress, requiring immense hard work.
Sun in the 10th house gives leadership, authority, and government favors.

Love Life:
Venus is the planet of love and relationships. 
Its placement in the 7th house heavily influences marriage and partnerships.
Mars in the 7th house can cause friction (Kuja Dosha/Manglik).

Current Transits (General):
When a malefic planet transits over your birth moon, it brings stress and mental anxiety (Sade Sati effect).
When benefic planets like Jupiter transit favorable houses, they bring opportunities and optimism.
